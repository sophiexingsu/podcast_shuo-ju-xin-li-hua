# Recording Checklist

## Before Recording

- Confirm episode brief and outline are current.
- Confirm guest or cohost names and pronunciation.
- Test microphone, headphones, camera if used, and recording app.
- Check room noise, notifications, battery, and storage.
- Confirm backup recording plan.
- Confirm release expectations if a guest is present.

## During Recording

- Slate episode title, date, participants, and backup status.
- Mark retakes verbally.
- Note timestamps for strong clips, errors, or follow-up.
- Pause for room tone if useful.

## After Recording

- Confirm files saved locally.
- Back up raw media outside Git.
- Add recording notes to the episode folder.
- Move the episode to post-production.
