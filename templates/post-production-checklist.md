# Post-Production Checklist

## Audio

- Clean major noise problems.
- Remove obvious mistakes, long dead air, and repeated retakes.
- Keep conversation natural unless the show format requires tight edits.
- Check loudness and final export settings.

## Transcript

- Generate transcript.
- Fix speaker names and obvious transcription errors.
- Mark unclear sections.
- Save approved transcript in the episode folder.

## Editorial

- Confirm title.
- Write show description.
- Write show notes.
- Add links and source credits.
- Confirm any sensitive claims are supported.

## Publishing

- Export final audio.
- Upload to hosting platform.
- Add title, description, transcript, episode art, and links.
- Schedule or publish.
- Save final public URL.

## QA

- Listen to intro, middle sample, and ending.
- Confirm no private notes, unreleased comments, or accidental side conversations remain.
- Confirm links work.
- Confirm cohosts approve release.
